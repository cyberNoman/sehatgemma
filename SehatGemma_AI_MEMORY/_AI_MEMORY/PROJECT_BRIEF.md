# 🏥 SEHATGEMMA — PROJECT BRIEF
> Last Updated: May 12, 2026 | Hackathon Deadline: May 18, 2026 (11:59 PM UTC)

---

## ONE SENTENCE
An offline-first, agentic AI nutrition guardian for Pakistani diabetes patients — powered by Gemma 4, running 100% on-device via Ollama, with zero cloud dependency.

---

## THE PROBLEM
- Pakistan has **31.4% diabetes prevalence** — highest in the world (IDF/WHO 2025)
- **33 million adults** affected, projected **70.2 million by 2050**
- Rural patients have **no internet, no endocrinologists, zero nutrition literacy**
- **46-70%** of patients have poor diabetes knowledge
- Existing AI health tools require cloud = useless in rural Pakistan

---

## THE SOLUTION
SehatGemma is NOT a traditional app. It is an **agentic health system** where:
- User speaks or photographs food
- Gemma 4 **decides the action** (analyze, log glucose, emergency, meal plan)
- Agent **executes the tool** (SQLite log, emergency alert, PROMPT guidelines)
- Response in **Urdu + English**, offline, private, encrypted
